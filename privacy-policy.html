3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under the NDPA:

  1. Consent (Section 25, NDPA): You provide explicit consent when you register for an Account, submit a loan application, authorise salary verification, or link your bank account through a third-party provider.
  2. Performance of Contract: Processing is necessary to perform our contractual obligations to you, including processing loan applications, disbursing funds, and managing repayments.
  3. Legal Obligation: We are required to process certain data to comply with regulatory obligations, including CBN KYC/AML requirements, the Money Laundering (Prevention and Prohibition) Act 2022, and credit bureau reporting obligations.
  4. Legitimate Interest: We process data for fraud prevention, security monitoring, credit risk assessment, and product improvement, where such processing does not override your fundamental rights and freedoms.

4. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Account management: Creating, verifying, and maintaining your Account;
  • Identity verification: Verifying your identity through BVN and other KYC processes;
  • Salary verification: Confirming your employment and salary details through Remita, Mono, or similar authorised third-party services;
  • Credit assessment: Evaluating your creditworthiness and determining loan eligibility, interest rates, and loan limits;
  • Loan processing: Processing loan applications, disbursing funds, and managing repayments;
  • Payment processing: Processing payments, direct debits, and refunds;
  • Credit bureau reporting: Reporting your loan performance to licensed credit bureaux as required by regulation;
  • Fraud prevention: Detecting, preventing, and investigating fraud, money laundering, and security incidents;
  • Communications: Sending transactional messages, loan reminders, repayment confirmations, and service updates;
  • Product improvement: Analysing usage patterns to improve the Platform, develop new features, and enhance user experience;
  • Legal compliance: Complying with applicable legal, regulatory, and supervisory obligations; and
  • Marketing: With your consent, sending promotional offers and marketing communications (you may opt out at any time).

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256);
  • Access controls: Role-based access control (RBAC) with the principle of least privilege;
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA): Required for all administrative and user access;
  • Key management: Secrets and credentials stored in Azure Key Vault with automatic rotation;
  • Network security: Web Application Firewall (WAF), network segmentation, and intrusion detection;
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring, logging, and alerting;
  • Incident response: Documented incident response plan with breach notification procedures; and
  • Regular assessments: Periodic vulnerability assessments and security audits.

9. Your Rights

Under the NDPA, NDPR, and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  1. Right of Access: You may request confirmation of whether we process your personal data and obtain a copy of such data.
  2. Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  3. Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal and regulatory retention obligations.
  4. Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  5. Right to Data Portability: You may request that we provide your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  6. Right to Object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests or processing for direct marketing purposes.
  7. Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.
  8. Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) if you believe your data protection rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@tootriv.com. We shall respond to your request within thirty (30) days. We may request additional information to verify your identity before processing your request.
