The Problem We're Solving

Millions of Nigerian workers live paycheck to paycheck. When emergencies strike, they have nowhere to turn.

💸

Paycheck-to-Paycheck Living

Unforeseen expenses — medical bills, school fees, family emergencies — create urgent liquidity needs for salaried workers.

🏦

Slow Bank Processes

Traditional banks offer slow, paperwork-heavy loan processes that can take weeks to approve even small amounts.

🦈

Predatory Lending

Informal money lenders exploit workers with predatory interest rates of 15-30% per month, trapping them in cycles of debt.

📵

Digital Divide

Many workers rely on basic feature phones. Existing digital lenders require smartphones and data, excluding the underbanked.

How TooTriv Works

Get a salary advance in 4 simple steps — no collateral, no paperwork, no hidden fees.

1

Register

Sign up with your BVN via USSD, Web, or Mobile. We auto-verify your identity in seconds.

2

Get Scored

Our AI-powered credit engine assesses your salary history, employer rating, and credit profile instantly.

3

Receive Funds

Approved loans are disbursed directly to your bank account in under 5 minutes — 24/7.

4

Auto-Repay

Repayment is deducted automatically from your salary via direct debit mandate on payday.
