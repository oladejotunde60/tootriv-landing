6. Lawful Bases for Processing

TooTriv processes Personal Data only where at least one of the following lawful bases under the NDPA applies:

Consent (NDPA s.25(1)(a))

Where you have provided explicit, informed, and freely given consent for a specific processing activity. Consent may be withdrawn at any time; withdrawal is prospective only and does not affect prior lawful processing.

Performance of Contract (NDPA s.25(1)(b))

Where processing is necessary to perform or prepare to perform a contract to which you are a party, including loan origination, account administration, disbursement, repayment management, and dispute resolution.

Legal Obligation (NDPA s.25(1)(c))

Where processing is required to comply with applicable law, including KYC/AML obligations under the MLPPA 2022, mandatory credit bureau reporting, CBN regulatory requirements, FCCPC obligations, tax reporting, and court orders.

Legitimate Interests (NDPA s.25(1)(f))

Where processing is necessary for TooTriv's legitimate interests (or those of a third party), provided such interests are not overridden by your fundamental rights. TooTriv relies on this basis for: fraud prevention and detection; risk management and portfolio monitoring; platform security; business analytics and service improvement; debt recovery and enforcement of contractual rights; and group-level reporting. A Legitimate Interests Assessment (LIA) is conducted and maintained on file for each such purpose.

Subject to applicable law and TooTriv's legitimate business and regulatory interests, you are entitled to exercise the following rights under the NDPA:

  1. Right of Access: You may request confirmation of whether we process your Personal Data and obtain a copy of such data.
  2. Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete Personal Data.
  3. Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your Personal Data, subject to our legal and regulatory retention obligations.
  4. Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit the processing of your Personal Data in certain circumstances.
  5. Right to Data Portability: You may request that we provide your Personal Data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  6. Right to Object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests or processing for direct marketing purposes.
  7. Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.
